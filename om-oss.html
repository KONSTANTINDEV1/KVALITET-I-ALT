A little about our vision and history.

Kvalitet i Alt is a quality assurance company that prides itself in ensuring that plans go according to a customers vision. Our vision is that our company will be a source of expertise and advice in our areas, and that we contribute to both the private and public sector, so that growth and new jobs are created and opened up for diversity in working life.
